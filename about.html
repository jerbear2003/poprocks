about pop rocks

the internet's most passionate Pop Tart appreciation project

🎯 Our Mission

Pop Rocks exists to celebrate the unparalleled superiority of Pop Tarts over Pop Rocks in a visually chaotic, psychedelic, and utterly delightful way. We believe that snack appreciation deserves bold colors, grungy aesthetics, and heart stickers. Lots of heart stickers.

This isn't just a website—it's a manifesto. Pop Tarts don't need defending. They speak for themselves through deliciousness, consistency, and pure vibes.

🎨 Design Philosophy

Every pixel of Pop Rocks is intentionally chaotic. We embraced the psychedelic, the grungy, the DIY zine aesthetic because Pop Tarts deserve nothing less than theatrical celebration.

  • 7 Dynamic Color Themes: Each page reload introduces a new psychedelic palette—no two visits are quite the same
  • Grungy Textures: Pure CSS grainy overlays create that worn, authentic feel
  • Sticker Aesthetics: Bold borders, tilted containers, peeling effects—it's like your favorite zine came to life
  • Heart Motif: Floating, pulsing, glowing hearts scatter across the site, celebrating love for the perfect pastry snack
  • Hand-Drawn Vibes: Web fonts like Caveat and Fredoka One reinforce the DIY, handcrafted aesthetic

✨ Features

🍓 Random Pop Tart

Can't decide? Let fate (and our random generator) choose your next delicious snack.

⚔️ Flavor Battles

Witness Pop Tarts utterly dominating Pop Rocks in head-to-head taste matchups with heart-based ratings.

🌈 Live Theming

Every page reload brings a new psychedelic color scheme. Warm, cool, earthy, electric—we got it all.

⌨️ Keyboard Magic

Press Ctrl+T to cycle themes, Ctrl+R to randomize, or unlock the Konami Code for psychedelic mode.

💔 Motion & Heart

Smooth animations, wobbling stickers, floating hearts, and celebratory effects make every interaction joyful.

📱 Mobile First

Responsive design ensures Pop Tart appreciation works beautifully on any device, anywhere, anytime.

⚙️ Built With

Pop Rocks is a pure vanilla web project—no frameworks, no bloat, just clean HTML, powerful CSS, and clever JavaScript.

  • HTML5: Semantic markup for accessibility and structure
  • CSS3: Custom properties for dynamic theming, Grid & Flexbox for layout, pure CSS animations
  • JavaScript (ES6+): Theme switching, random image selection, keyboard shortcuts, Easter eggs
  • Web Fonts: Fredoka One, Caveat, Permanent Marker for authentic zine aesthetic
  • SVG: Beautiful, scalable Pop Tart illustrations

❤️ Made By

Pop Rocks was born from an unhealthy obsession with Pop Tarts and a passion for psychedelic web design. It's a love letter to both snacks and chaos.

Created with ❤️ and absolute conviction that Pop Tarts always win. Every. Single. Time.
